Apps & Tools

Free desktop and mobile applications for forest operations accounting, timber measurements, log rules, conversions, and sawmill analysis.

Available

LiVT — Log in Vermont

Calculate financial results for a logging business on a weekly, monthly, annual, or per-job basis. Throughput accounting built for logging.

Available

iSawVT

Sawmill production analysis. Desktop and mobile versions for evaluating log-to-lumber recovery, overrun, and mill profitability.

Available

BARK

Biomass, accounting, and resource knowledge. Desktop and mobile app for biomass operations tracking and analysis.

Available

Northern Logger App

Mobile companion for Northern Logger magazine. Timber weight tables, log rules, and field reference tools.

Available

Timber Tools

Log rule calculators, unit conversions, and timber measurement utilities in a single mobile app.

PATH 3.0

Production analysis for timber harvesting. Track and analyze logging productivity metrics.

LC Benchmark

Benchmarking tool for comparing logging operation performance against industry data.

BOLT Business Plan

Business planning app for logging operations. Build and evaluate business plans with forestry-specific financial models.

Available

Surewood

Forest products and measurements converter. Quick conversions between board feet, cords, tons, and other forest product units.

Cut Mark
